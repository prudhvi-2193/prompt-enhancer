## üéØ How It Works

1. **Enter Your Text**: Type your simple request or idea in the input area
2. **Select Type**: Choose the prompt type that best matches your needs:
   - **General**: All-purpose enhancement
   - **Code/Technical**: For programming and technical tasks
   - **Creative Writing**: For stories, articles, and creative content
   - **Analysis/Research**: For data analysis and research tasks
   - **Business/Professional**: For business documents and proposals
3. **Enhance**: Click the "Enhance Prompt" button or press `Ctrl+Enter`
4. **Copy & Use**: Copy the enhanced prompt and use it with your favorite AI assistant
